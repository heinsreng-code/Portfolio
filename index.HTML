About Me

Hello, I’m Hein Min Oo.

I am a civil engineering and facilities management professional with experience in construction, infrastructure, BIM coordination, and maintenance operations. My current academic focus is learning how AI can improve construction workflows and facility lifecycle management.

Through this course, I am connecting classroom learning with practical FM challenges such as asset information quality, preventive maintenance readiness, digital handover, technician-friendly documentation, and decision-making from reliable data.

Portfolio Focus Areas

  • AI adoption in construction and facilities management
  • BIM-to-FM workflows and asset data quality
  • CMMS, BAS, GIS, and digital handover coordination
  • Weekly article reflections and applied learning
  • Practical recommendations for industry use
