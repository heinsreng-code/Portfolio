About Me

Engineering experience shaped by construction, facilities, and digital transformation.

I am Hein Min Oo, a construction and facilities management professional based in Charlotte, North Carolina. My background combines civil engineering, infrastructure construction, campus project coordination, BIM-enabled workflows, and facilities operations support.

Professional Summary

Bridging project delivery and facility operations.

My professional focus is to connect planning, design, construction, maintenance, and digital information so that projects are easier to manage and buildings are easier to operate after turnover.

I currently support campus renovation, infrastructure, and maintenance-related initiatives in a university facilities environment. My work includes project coordination, design review support, field verification, documentation control, BIM/ACC support, asset information review, and communication with maintenance supervisors, technical staff, designers, contractors, and internal stakeholders.

Before working in facilities and campus project support, I built a strong technical foundation through major infrastructure and underground construction projects in Singapore and overseas. Those projects included MRT stations, tunnels, deep excavations, piling works, reinforced concrete structures, utility diversions, and coordination with clients, consultants, subcontractors, and regulatory authorities.

AI in Construction & FM

Why this course matters to my career path.

This portfolio is part of my AI in Construction and Facilities Management coursework. The course helped me think more deeply about how AI can support real industry needs, including project planning, document review, automation, risk awareness, data quality, and facility lifecycle management.

My main interest is using AI in a practical and responsible way. For me, the most valuable use of AI is not just creating faster outputs.
